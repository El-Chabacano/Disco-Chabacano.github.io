Gorillaz
The Now Now

THE BEST ALBUM OF 2018

The Now Now is the sixth studio album by British virtual band Gorillaz. It was released on June 29, 2018, under the record label of Parlophone and Warner Bros. Recording began during the North American section of the Humanz Tour in late 2017.Filming began during the North American section of the Humanz Tour in late 2017, with fewer collaborations than usual due to criticism that the previous album, Humanz, had too many guest artists. According to Gorillaz co-creator Damon Albarn, the album was recorded so fast so that the band could have more material to play at future concerts.
